1. Vary the Layout (Break the Rigid Grid)A repetitive $3 \times 2$ grid of identically sized boxes is a hallmark signature of an AI template. To make it look truly human-designed, introduce a Bento Grid layout where some boxes are larger or wider than others based on importance.Make your highest-value features—like Deep Analytics (maybe with a mock graph preview) and Fully Customizable—take up 2 columns of width.Keep smaller features like Blazing Fast or Secure by Default as smaller, single-column boxes.2. Inject Real UI Previews (Show, Don't Just Tell)Right now, the boxes are text-heavy with simple icons. Human designers prefer to show the product working:Fully Customizable: Instead of just text, have a small interactive or floating preview of a profile switching color themes or showing a toggle switch moving.Deep Analytics: Drop a tiny, minimalist glowing line chart or bar chart inside the box underneath the text to make it look active.Unlimited Links: Show a visual stack of three small link buttons, with a little drag-and-drop hand indicator icon next to one.3. Upgrade the Borders and BackgroundsThe current boxes use flat, solid borders, which look a bit rigid. You can make them look incredibly modern with CSS changes:Add Radial Gradient Glows: Make the border or background highlight slightly when a user hovers their mouse over a specific card.Soft Border Colors: Instead of standard grey borders, use a very faint purple or tinted border (rgba(168, 85, 247, 0.15)) so they blend organically into the dark theme.4. Humanize the CopyAI text tends to use generic punchlines like "Built for the culture. Everything you need to stand out. Nothing you don't." Shifting this slightly makes it feel like an authentic indie hacker brand:Heading Idea: "Your corner of the internet, customized." or "Ditch the generic link-in-bio."Subheading Idea: "A beautiful, blazing-fast profile built to showcase your links, style, and stats without subscription fatigue."5. Make the "$9 Lifetime Premium" PopYour pricing is an incredible feature, but right now it is buried as just another box in the corner. Give this card a distinct visual treatment:Add a slight purple gradient background to just that box to draw the eye.Give it a small, glowing green or purple text badge that says "Best Value" or "One-time payment".
